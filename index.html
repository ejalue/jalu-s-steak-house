THE JALU'S

We pride ourselves on selecting every cut of meat from the finest sources, ensuring exceptional quality and deliciousness in every bite. With a warm atmosphere and friendly service, we promise to provide you with an unforgettable dining experience every time you visit Jalu's Meatery. MENUS

A cut of beef from the rib section, known for its rich marbling of fat. This fat provides exceptional flavor and tenderness when cooked. This cut is usually thick, with a visible strip of fat running along it. Cooking perfectly allows this fat to melt, enhancing the flavor and tenderness of the meat.

Beef cut from the breast. This meat has a high fat content and is generally cooked slowly and at low heat to create a tender and flavorful texture. Usually served with barbecue sauce or special seasonings.

Beef cuts from the hip and back. It has a grainy but tender texture with a slight marbling of fat, Served with sauce and sides such as potatoes or roasted vegetables. Sirloin steak is available in a variety of varieties, all offering rich flavor and mouth-watering tenderness.

A cut of beef from the upper back that is very tender and less fatty. Famous for its deliciousness, it is often a choice at Jalu's Meatery.

A cut of beef cut from the ribs, it has a unique appearance due to its long bones resembling a North American Indian battle axe. Grilled to maintain moisture and deliciousness of the meat. This cut is popular at Jalu's Meatery and is a Main course.

A cut of beef from Wagyu cattle, renowned for its high marbling and rich flavor. It is usually cooked carefully to maintain the tenderness and deliciousness of the meat. Often considered one of the best steaks at Jalu's Meatery.

This piece of chicken meat can be thickly cut chicken breast or other parts of the chicken which are processed into steaks which are cooked like beef steak, often grilled or smoked with Jalu' Meatery's signature herbs and spices.

Pieces of beef cut and prepared for cooking. This cut can come from various parts of the cow, such as ribeye, tenderloin, sirloin, striploin, or other parts. Steaks are usually grilled, or cooked in other ways to reach the desired level of doneness.
